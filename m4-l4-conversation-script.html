The AI Setter Conversation Script

How a well-prompted Claude handles a lead from "just got the magnet" to "booking link sent" — in your voice.

Stage 1 — Open & qualify
🤖
AI Setter (in your voice)
Hey! Here's your free guide 👇 [link] — it covers exactly how to set this up step by step. Quick question before you dive in: are you currently running paid ads, doing organic content, or a bit of both?
Delivers magnet immediately. First qualifier is casual, not interrogating.
👤
Lead
Both! I run Meta ads and post on Instagram but nothing's really converting right now 😩
Stage 2 — Deepen + diagnose
🤖
AI Setter
Ugh, the worst feeling — spending on ads and posting consistently and still hearing crickets. What are you actually selling? Like what's the main offer people are supposed to be clicking through to?
Empathy first. Then a natural second qualifier — what's the offer.
👤
Lead
I have a $3k coaching programme for health coaches. I get clicks but nobody buys or even books calls.
🤖
AI Setter
OK so you've got a real offer with real value and you ARE getting clicks — that's actually a great sign. That tells me the issue is probably happening between the click and the conversation, not in the ad itself. How are you currently following up with leads who click?
Reframes positively. Third qualifier — what's the follow-up system (or lack of).
👤
Lead
Honestly I'm not really… I just wait and hope they book 😂
Stage 3 — Position + CTA
🤖
AI Setter
That's actually the exact gap this system fixes — you're doing everything right up to the DM, then leaving the sale to chance. What we build replaces that gap with an AI setter that qualifies and follows up automatically so you're not the bottleneck. Based on what you've shared, I think a quick 20-min call would actually be really useful — I could show you exactly what the setup would look like for your coaching funnel specifically. Want me to send you a link?
Positions the system as the solution to their exact problem. Soft close — "want me to send you a link?"
→ If yes: sends Calendly link + tags as HOT LEAD
The 6 rules of AI setter conversation
💬
One question at a time
Never stack two questions. Ask one, wait for the answer, then ask the next. Stacking = interrogation.
❤️
Empathy before advice
Acknowledge their situation before positioning the solution. "Ugh, the worst feeling" before the pivot.
🔄
Reframe negatives
Turn their problem into a sign of potential. "You're getting clicks — that's actually great" flips the frame.
🎯
Personalise the CTA
Reference their specific situation in the close. "For your coaching funnel" not "for businesses like yours".
🚪
Soft CTA not hard push
"Want me to send you a link?" converts better than "Book here." Soft = less resistance.
🏷️
Tag on every response
Every answer they give updates their lead tag in the CRM. Hot lead by message 5 or moved to nurture.
