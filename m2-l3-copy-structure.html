Desire-First Copy Formula

Lead with what they want. Not what you built. Not how it works. What THEY get.

D
Desire — Lead with the outcome they want
Open with the result they're after. Speak to what they wish was already true. This is your hook — it must land in 3 seconds or they're gone.
"How to get qualified leads booking calls in your DMs while you sleep — without posting content every day"
A
Agitate — Name their current reality
Describe what's actually happening for them right now. The frustration, the thing that isn't working, the gap between where they are and where they want to be.
"You're posting 3x a day, running ads that eat budget, and still waking up to zero DMs from qualified people who actually want to buy..."
S
Solution — Present the mechanism as the bridge
NOW you introduce the how. The AI setter, the ManyChat system, the tool. But position it as the bridge to their desire — not the feature being sold.
"I built a system that captures leads from ads and organic content, qualifies them automatically in DMs, and books calls — while I'm not even online"
C
CTA — One specific next step
Tell them exactly what to do and why to do it now. Comment a keyword, click a link, DM a word. One action only — never two.
"Comment 'LEADS' and I'll send you the exact setup I use — takes 48 hours to build"
Before & After — The Operator Tone Shift
✗ Mechanism-first (kills conversions)
"My AI-powered ManyChat + Claude API integration automates your lead qualification and DM follow-up sequences..."
Cold traffic doesn't know what Claude API is. They don't care about the mechanism. CPAs skyrocket because you've lost them at "AI-powered".
✓ Desire-first (attracts buyers)
"Stop answering the same DM questions manually. I'll show you how to have a system that qualifies and books leads for you — in your voice."
Speaks to the pain (repetitive manual DMs) and the desire (qualified leads booked automatically). Mechanism is implied, not the headline.
⚡
The AI positioning rule: Don't lead with "AI" to cold traffic — they don't know what AI mentors or AI setters are yet. Sell the transformation. The AI is the mechanism, not the promise. Once they're in your world and they understand what you do — then you can talk about AI. On cold ads, lead with the outcome and mention AI as proof, not as the hook.
