The Lead Magnet Ad Flow

Comment → trigger → DM → opt-in. This is the top-of-funnel system that feeds everything else.

1
📣
Ad or Post
Paid lead gen ad or organic content post with keyword CTA
2
💬
Comment Trigger
Person comments the keyword. ManyChat detects it instantly.
3
📩
Auto DM Sent
ManyChat fires a DM in seconds. Lead magnet delivered automatically.
4
🤖
AI Setter Kicks In
Claude API takes over. Qualifies the lead through conversation.
5
📞
Call or Sale
Qualified lead books a call or buys direct. Tagged in CRM.
✗ Link Click Ads
Traffic to a page
Higher friction. Cold traffic bounces. No relationship built. You get a lead but they don't know you. Hard to follow up.
✓ Comment to DM
Conversation started
Lower friction. Algorithm rewards comments. Lead is already in your DMs. AI can qualify immediately. Warmer from step one.
What makes a good lead magnet
Solves ONE specific, immediate problem
Fast to consume (under 10 minutes)
Creates a natural "what's next?" moment
Speaks directly to your ideal buyer's biggest frustration
Title formula: "How to [desired outcome] without [common objection]"
ManyChat keyword setup
Choose a short, memorable keyword (eg. "LEADS", "GUIDE", "FREE")
Connect to your Instagram and Facebook Page
Welcome message → deliver lead magnet → ask first qualifying question
Tag leads by source (paid ad vs organic post)
Connect to CRM via Zapier or native integration
⚡
Why this works at scale: The moment someone comments your keyword, ManyChat fires a DM in seconds. Your AI setter then handles the conversation 24/7. You wake up to qualified leads already in your pipeline — without being online. The comment also signals the algorithm to show your post to more people, making your organic reach compound.
