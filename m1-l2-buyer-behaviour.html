Buyer Behaviour Map

Stop targeting demographics. Target behaviour. Here's how to spot a real buyer.

✗ Don't target this (demographics)
🎂
Age 25–45 female
Tells you almost nothing about buying intent or budget
📍
Location-based targeting
Geography doesn't mean they have money or a problem you solve
🎯
"Interested in entrepreneurship"
Millions of people are "interested" — interest ≠ action taker
👥
Broad job titles
"Business owner" is too wide — doesn't tell you what they actually do
✓ Target this (behaviour)
📣
They already have an audience
Posting content, growing followers, running a community — they have distribution
🛒
They already have an offer
Course, coaching, membership, service — they're already selling something
💳
They're spending money to grow
Running ads, buying tools, paying for coaching — they invest in their business
🔥
They're actively searching for solutions
Watching competitor ads, clicking on similar products, engaging with peers in the space
The 3 Buyer Signals That Matter
01
Has an audience
They already have people paying attention. Your tool makes their content + ads work harder.
02
Has an offer
They're already selling. They need more leads, better conversion — not validation that selling is possible.
03
Spends money
Investing in their business already. They understand ROI. They won't flinch at $497.
🚫
The Brokie Filter — Use It In Your Copy
Language in your ads and content naturally self-selects. When you write "if you're already running ads but they're not converting..." — beginners with no ad budget self-select out. When you say "your $5k/month coaching offer..." — people charging $97 know it's not for them. Your language is your filter. Use it deliberately.
