Short Form Video for Lead Gen

Not virality. Not views. Buyers. Here's how to structure every video to attract the right person and push them to your DMs.

The CTA
Specific action + specific reason. Comment keyword → DM.
Hook rules (first 3 seconds)
Call out your specific person by situation, not by title
Buyer hooks → outcome-led or problem-specific. Not entertainment hooks.
On-screen text = tells the algorithm who to show it to. Use it deliberately.
Pattern interrupt in the first frame. Blurry, unexpected angle, bold statement.
The one insight rule
One concept, taught well, beats five concepts taught shallowly every time
The insight should make them think "I never heard it explained that way"
Move positions, change expressions, use hands — keep engagement in longer videos
CTA formula
Specific action: "Comment [keyword]" not "follow for more"
Specific reason: "and I'll send you the [exact thing]"
Create urgency: "it takes 48 hours to set up and it works forever"
4 buyer-attracting hook types
Pain Hook
"If you're posting every day and still not getting qualified leads in your DMs..."
Outcome Hook
"How I get 30+ qualified leads in my DMs every week without posting more than 3x"
Controversy Hook
"Stop building a personal brand. Build a system instead. Here's why."
Story Hook
"I woke up to 47 DMs from qualified leads. Here's the one post that caused it."
⚡
500 right views vs 500,000 wrong views. A Reel watched by 500 people who are already running ads and spending money on tools will generate more revenue than one with 500k views from people who are just interested in entrepreneurship. Optimise for buyer specificity, not viral reach.
