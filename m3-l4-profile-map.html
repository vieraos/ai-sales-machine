Profile Conversion Map

Your profile is a silent sales funnel. Before anyone DMs you — it's already pre-qualifying, pre-selling, and pre-trusting them.

📌
Pinned Posts
First things a cold visitor reads
Pin 1: Your best lead gen post. The one with the most comments and DMs. Put your keyword CTA at the top.
Pin 2: Your biggest proof post. Best client result, biggest transformation, most convincing testimonial.
Pin 3: Your personal story post. Why you, why this, why now. The "I feel like I know her" post.
✅
Authority Signals
What premium buyers look for before DMing
Consistent posting: Active feed = real business. Gaps signal unreliability to cold visitors.
Real results: Not "I teach this" — "my students did this". Proof in the feed, not just in highlights.
Confident positioning: You know who you help and who you don't. No vague "I help entrepreneurs".
Engagement: Replies to comments. Active in stories. You're present, not just broadcasting.
The bio formula — 4 lines, each does a job
Line 1
What you do + who for
"I help coaches & digital product sellers build AI-powered lead systems"
Line 2
Proof + authority signal
"$2M+ in digital product sales · 500+ students · Founder @VIERAOS"
Line 3
The specific desire your audience has
"Leads in your DMs while you sleep 👇"
Line 4
One CTA with the lead magnet keyword
"Comment 'LEADS' on my latest post for the free setup guide"
