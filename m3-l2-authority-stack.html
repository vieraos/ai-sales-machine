The Authority Content Stack

Three types of content. Each does a different job. Rotate them intentionally and your profile converts cold visitors on autopilot.

Type 1
🔥
Trust Builders
Builds parasocial connection. People feel like they know you before they ever DM you. This is the "I feel like she's talking directly to me" content.
Behind the scenes of your actual work
Personal story that ties to your offer
Day-in-the-life / how you actually work
Opinion or hot take on your industry
Type 2
🏆
Proof Posts
Builds credibility with people who don't know you yet. Client results, testimonials, screenshots, revenue. Cold traffic needs this before they trust you.
Client result with context (not just a stat)
Testimonial screenshot or video
Before/after transformation story
Revenue or outcome milestone
Type 3
🎯
Lead Generators
The CTA-driven posts that move people to action. These are your comment-to-DM posts. Every lead gen post feeds ManyChat and builds your pipeline.
"Comment X for [specific free resource]"
Value post with keyword CTA at the end
Problem-agitation post → "DM me X"
Challenge or quiz → "Comment to find out"
