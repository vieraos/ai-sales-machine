ABO [TEST]
When to use it
You're launching a new creative or angle
You don't have proven winners yet
You want to see which specific ad is performing
You're iterating on an existing winner
CBO [SCALE]
When to use it
You have 3+ creatives with proven ROAS
You want Meta to find the best audiences
You're ready to increase budget significantly
You have your numbers locked (CPP, AOV, threshold)
⚡
The rule that saves your ad budget: Never put money into CBO until ABO has given you proven winners. CBO amplifies whatever you give it — give it untested ads and you're just spending faster to lose faster. Build winners in ABO. Scale winners in CBO.
