The Hybrid Handoff Model

AI handles everything it can. Human steps in only when it matters. Here's exactly where the line is.

🤖
AI Lane
Runs 24/7 without you
First response
Delivers lead magnet, welcomes the lead, fires within seconds of the comment or DM
Qualification questions
Asks 3–5 qualifying questions naturally across the conversation. Tags the CRM on every answer.
First objection handling
"I need to think about it" / "Is this the right time?" — Claude handles the standard first-response objections
Low-ticket payment link
Sends payment link for offers under $500 directly in the conversation when lead is qualified
Booking link for calls
Sends Calendly/GHL booking link when lead hits the high-ticket qualification threshold
Nurture tagging
Leads not ready now tagged for follow-up sequence. Not lost — just deferred.
HANDOFF
🧑‍💼
Human Lane
You step in strategically
High-ticket close
Offers over $2k — you take the call. The AI has already qualified them so you're talking to buyers, not browsers.
Complex objections
Edge cases, unusual situations, nuanced hesitations that fall outside Claude's scripted responses
Relationship moments
VIP clients, referrals, people you already know — human touch adds to what the AI started
Upsell conversations
After a low-ticket sale, a personal follow-up to pitch the next tier dramatically increases LTV
Difficult situations
Refund requests, unhappy customers, anything requiring empathy beyond a script

The goal isn't to remove yourself from sales — it's to make sure every conversation you have is worth having. AI handles the volume so that when you show up in a DM, it's to close a qualified buyer who already understands your offer, not to answer "what do you do?" for the 400th time.
