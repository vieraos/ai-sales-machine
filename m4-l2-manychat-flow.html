ManyChat Flow Architecture

Step by step — from keyword trigger to lead captured in your CRM. Build this once. It runs forever.

💬
Trigger
Keyword detected in comment or DM
e.g. someone comments "LEADS" on your post or sends it as a DM
↓
📩
Message 1 — Instant DM
Welcome + deliver lead magnet
"Hey! Here's your [lead magnet] 👇 [link]. Quick question before I send the full thing..."
↓
❓
Message 2 — First qualifier
Ask the first qualifying question
"Are you currently running ads or focused on organic right now?" [Button: Ads | Organic | Both]
↓
🏷️
Action — Tag lead
Tag based on answer + notify CRM
Tag: "running-ads" or "organic-only" or "both". Fire Zapier webhook to GHL/ActiveCampaign.
↓
🤖
Condition — Hand to AI
Pass conversation to Claude API setter
ManyChat passes the full conversation thread to your Claude API integration. AI takes over qualification.
↓
📞
Outcome
Call booked or sale made or nurtured
AI sends booking link for qualified leads. Payment link for low-ticket. Nurture tag for not yet ready.

Lead magnet delivery message

AI setter handoff to Claude API
🏷️
Tagging Strategy — This is How You Know Who to Follow Up With
Tag every lead by source (paid-ad, organic-post), by qualifier answer (running-ads, organic-only), and by status (cold-lead, warm-lead, hot-lead, booked-call, sold). These tags feed your retargeting audiences and tell your AI setter how to treat each person differently.
⚡
Build it once. It runs forever. A ManyChat flow built properly this week will still be capturing and qualifying leads from posts you publish in 6 months. Every piece of content you ever create from now on feeds this same system. That's the compounding advantage of setting it up right.
