The anatomy of a lead-generating post
Part 1
The Hook
Stops the scroll in 1–2 lines. Desire-led or pattern interrupt. Makes them need to read more.
"Stop posting content every day trying to get leads. Here's what actually works instead."
Part 2
The Value Tease
Give one real insight. Make them feel like they just got something. Build desire for the full thing.
"The secret is one post, set up with a keyword trigger, that generates DMs automatically for weeks..."
Part 3
The CTA
Specific keyword. Specific action. One thing only. "Comment X" always beats "link in bio".
"Comment 'LEADS' and I'll send you the exact post template + ManyChat setup I use"
