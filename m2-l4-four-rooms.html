The Four Room House

Same message. Four completely different visual worlds. Zero ad cannibalization.

The Andromeda Principle
Meta's algorithm reads your creatives like fingerprints. If two ads look the same, it picks one and kills the other — you pay double and get half the results. The fix: one concept, four completely different visual executions. The algorithm sees four distinct ads and your reach multiplies.
Your 4 visual rooms — same message, different fingerprint
🎤
Talking Head
Direct to camera. Natural, organic style. No heavy editing. You talking to camera like you talk to your community.
Educator / Coach
🎬

Builder / SaaS
⚠️ What cannibalization looks like vs four rooms
✗ Same visual fingerprint
Three talking head videos with the same background, same outfit, same style. Meta picks one. The others die and you've wasted the budget.
→
✓ Four room strategy
Talking head + B-roll + static + UGC all running simultaneously. Meta reads them as four completely different ads. All four compete and you get data on which room wins.
Static Image Copy Rules — The Only Rules That Matter
Headline: max 6 words. Desire-outcome led. No questions.
CTA banner: max 8 words. One clear action.
If you can't read it in 2 seconds — it's too cluttered
Image speaks to your ideal buyer's world (before or after state)
Less text = more conversions. Every time.

⚡
Your brand archetype = your starting room. Educator/Coach → warm lifestyle talking head. Disruptor → bold typography static. Builder/SaaS → dark UI + screen recordings. Creator → UGC + B-roll. Start in the room that fits your brand, then test the others once you have a winner.
