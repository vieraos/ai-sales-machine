The 3 Traffic Buckets

Every lead you ever get comes from one of these three places. Pick your path.

🎬
Organic
You create content. You build the audience over time. Free to run — costs your time and consistency.
⏱ Costs time
💸
Paid Ads
You run ads. You buy the audience immediately. Fast to scale — costs money to run.
💰 Costs money
🤝
Borrowed
Collabs, affiliates, features. You borrow someone else's audience to grow your own.
🔗 Costs relationships
All roads lead to
Your DMs & Funnel
Lead captured → AI setter takes over
📞
Call Booked
AI qualifies → human closer takes over → high ticket sale
🛒
Direct Sale
AI sends payment link → low to mid ticket product sold in DMs
📧
List Built
Lead magnet delivered → tagged in CRM → nurtured to sale
⚡
The one rule: Pick one primary traffic source and stick to it until it's working. Organic validates your message for free. Paid scales what's already working. Borrowed amplifies both. Don't try to run all three at once — you'll do none of them well.
