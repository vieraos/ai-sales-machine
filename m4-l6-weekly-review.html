The Weekly Review Loop

A system without a review loop is just guessing on repeat. This is the 30-minute weekly habit that compounds your results.

Monday
📊
Check the numbers
Leads in, conversion rate, where leads dropped off. Tag pipeline updated?
Tuesday
💬
Read the conversations
Spot where Claude is losing people. What question killed the thread? What got the best response?
Wednesday
✏️
Update the prompt
One tweak to the system prompt based on what you saw. One thing at a time — never wholesale rewrite.
Friday
🔁
End-of-week log
Record what changed, what improved, what to test next week. System compounds week on week.
↺ Repeats every week. Each cycle the system gets smarter.
Weekly review — 3 areas to audit
Conversation quality
Where did leads stop responding?
Which questions got the best engagement?
Did the objection handling work or stall?
Did Claude stay in your voice or drift generic?
Pipeline numbers
Total new leads this week
Leads qualified vs leads cold
Booking link clicked vs booked
Sales made (low + high ticket)
Traffic sources
Which ad creative sent the most qualified leads?
Which organic post triggered the most ManyChat flows?
Paid vs organic lead quality — is there a difference?
Any traffic source generating unqualified volume?
⚡ The improvement compound cycle
Read dead threads
→
Identify drop-off point
→
Rewrite that response only
→
Run for 7 days
→
Compare conversion rate
→
Keep or revert
⚡
30 minutes a week is all this takes. Most people build the system and never look at it again. The ones who review it weekly end up with a setter that converts at 3–5x the rate of set-and-forget. The conversations the AI is having are the most valuable data your business generates. Read them.
